Welcome to The Sports Time Machine!

What is this tool?

This tool is designed to help users generate customized reports on sports games, focusing on leagues such as the NBA, NFL, NHL, and MLB. It uses advanced AI to create narratives based on user preferences for date, narrative style, and article length.

How to use this tool?

  • Select a Date: Choose the date for which you want to generate a report. This should be the date when the games you are interested in occurred.
  • Choose Leagues: Select one or more sports leagues you are interested in. You can choose from NBA, NFL, NHL, and MLB. To select multiple leagues, check multiple boxes.
  • Narrative Style: Choose the style in which you want the report to be written. Options include 'Biased', 'Statistics', and 'Narrative'. This will determine how the information is presented in your report.
  • Article Length: Select whether you want a short, medium, or long report. This will affect how detailed the report will be.
  • Submit: Once you have filled out all the options, click the submit button to generate your report. The report will be displayed on the next page.

We hope you find this tool helpful and easy to use as you explore sports game reporting with tailored insights!
